Challenges, Projects, Battles, and Leadboards: Are you interested?

Books and lectures are great, but the best way to improve your skills is to practice and solve problems in real time. This website directs you to various tools you can use to enhance your skills. With Codewars, you can work to solve a vast library of coding challenges in multiple languages, join clans, and compete against other clans for kata supremacy. LeetCode gives you the opportunity to achieve algorithm and data structure mastery. Hack The Box provides an environment to explore penetration testing, network analysis, vulnerability exploits, and more. Want to 'gamify' your learning? With CSSBattle, you can battle other users where quality and speed determine who is victorious in a given design challenge.

Unlock your potential with the websites below

  • LeetCode
  • Hack The Box
  • CSSBattle

Bottom Line

Whether you're trying to ace a project at school, rock a technical interview, defeat imposter syndrome, or distinguish yourself among your peers, the key is to learn by doing. Practicing your skills through the interactive challenges and projects provided by these resources will give you the experience and knowledge you need to realize your goals.
